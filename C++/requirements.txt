-------------------Train Ticket Booking System Requirements-----------------------------------

1. User Registration
- The system must provide user registration functionality, allowing users to create accounts.
- Users must be able to sign in with their credentials securely.

2. Train Search
- The system must offer a comprehensive train search feature, enabling users to find trains based on various criteria.
- Users should be able to search for trains by specifying departure and arrival stations, travel dates, and class of travel.

3. Seat Availability
- The system must provide users with the ability to check the availability of seats on a specific train for a given date.
- Users should be able to verify the availability of seats in different classes, such as economy, business, and first class.

4. Ticket Booking
- Users must be able to book tickets for their chosen train and class.
- The booking process should be user-friendly and intuitive, guiding users through the required steps.
- Users should receive a confirmation of their booking after successful payment.

5. Payment Processing
- The system must ensure secure and reliable payment processing for ticket bookings.
- Payment options should include various methods such as credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and net banking.
- Users' payment information should be handled with the highest level of security and compliance with industry standards.

6. Ticket Cancellation
- Users should have the ability to cancel their booked tickets through the system.
- The system should specify rules and procedures for ticket cancellation, including refund policies and associated fees.

7. Booking History
- The system should maintain a comprehensive booking history for each user.
- Users should be able to view their past bookings, including details such as travel dates, train information, and class of travel.
- Historical booking data should be easily accessible and organized.

8. Train Schedule
- The system must display detailed train schedules, including departure and arrival times, for all available trains.
- Users should have access to up-to-date information about train schedules to plan their journeys effectively.
- The train schedule information should be accurate and regularly updated.
